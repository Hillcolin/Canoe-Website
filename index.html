About Us

Misty Moose canoes is a small, Canadian business specializing in the design of handcrafted canoes. We offer a range of durable, high-quality canoes built to last. Our products are made to order, which ensures that each canoe is unique and fits your needs. We also provide accessories and canoe repair to help ensure your canoes stays in top condition.

Meet the Team

Rob Fisher - Founder

Born in 1961, Rob had to walk around a lake to school going up hill both ways. After 5 years of this, Rob made his own canoe, which was named the Misty Moose, thus beginning the company. In these canoes, Rob sailed the high sea, battling sharks and catching whales for his family, all at the age of 10.
